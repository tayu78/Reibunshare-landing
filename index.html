Get Started

Even if you have studied English words for long time with vocabulary books, it is difficult to remember all of them since vocabulary is too many.

the example sentences vocabulary books have are sometimes useful , but many sentence is not useful in daily conversation.

Sometimes you might tried to create your own sentence for each words to memorize easily, but you may think you wish you could see the sentence the native speaker made.

this app should help to solve this kind of problem.
